Nas & DJ Premier – Light‑Years

This year has been a goldmine for hip‑hop heads, with countless great releases dropping. The one that I — along with many other enthusiasts of the genre — had been anticipating most was the long‑awaited Nas & DJ Premier album. And now, it’s finally here!

Light‑Years consists of 15 tracks filled with hard‑hitting drums, sample‑heavy beats, and the razor‑sharp cuts that have made Premier a god‑like figure in hip‑hop production. Meanwhile, Nas delivers his signature, Harvard‑level wordplay and bars that serve as a true love letter to the culture. After more than two decades of waiting, this album feels like a victory lap for two of the greatest of all time.
